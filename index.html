ABOUT US

Pratha Hospital

Pratha Hospital (PAARTH BYOUIN PVT. LTD.) was founded by Dr. Ajit Kumar Rawat, a renowned expert in the field of Gastroenterology. The hospital is a specialized center, primarily focused on the diagnosis and treatment of Gastroenterology and Hepatology conditions.

  • Our goal is to help patients recover quickly and regain their quality of life through personalized care and advanced medical expertise.

ICU

A five-bedded Intensive Care Unit (ICU) equipped with ventilators represents a crucial component of advanced medical care, providing life-saving support for critically ill patients. Ventilators are essential machines that assist patients in breathing when they are unable to do so effectively on their own due to severe illness, injury, or respiratory failure. This ICU setup ensures constant monitoring of patients and access to specialized medical equipment for emergencies. With trained medical staff and a focus on hygiene and safety, the ICU offers a controlled environment where patients receive personalized and intensive care, improving their chances of recovery.

ambulance and pharmacy services

Our hospital is proud to offer both ambulance and pharmacy services to ensure comprehensive and accessible care for our patients.
Ambulance Services: With a well-equipped ambulance service, we provide timely and efficient transportation for patients in need of urgent medical attention. Our ambulances are staffed with trained professionals and fitted with essential medical equipment, ensuring patient safety and stabilization during transit.
Pharmacy Services: Our in-house pharmacy ensures that patients have convenient access to a wide range of medications and health supplies. Our pharmacy team works closely with healthcare providers to offer accurate medication guidance and support, prioritizing patient safety and well-being.

Equipped with the latest technology

Our hospital is equipped with the latest technology to ensure prompt and accurate diagnosis of digestive problems. With advanced diagnostic tools and cutting-edge medical equipment, we aim to provide comprehensive evaluations and precise results for gastrointestinal health. These technologies allow us to identify conditions efficiently, enabling tailored treatment plans and improving patient outcomes.
